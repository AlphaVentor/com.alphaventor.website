Sizing

Size It Once, Build It Right – No Costly Surprises Later

Auditing Your Process with Ruthless Precision

Before any euro is committed, we dive deep into your existing or planned process streams, energy balances, and operational constraints. Using proven thermodynamic modeling, real-world data reconciliation, and scenario analysis, we uncover hidden inefficiencies, oversizing traps, or underestimated peak loads that typical feasibility studies miss – whether it’s a district heating network, an ORC waste-heat recovery unit, a datacenter cooling loop, or a compressed-gas facility.

Defining the Key Metrics That Actually Matter

We deliver a crystal-clear sizing report with verifiable key performance indicators: exact thermal duty, optimum turbine or compressor power, realistic turndown ratios, annual energy yield, and lifecycle cost curves. These aren’t theoretical numbers – they become the unbreakable foundation for specifications, tenders, and contracts, ensuring your plant is neither underpowered nor wastefully oversized, but perfectly dimensioned for performance and profitability from day one.
